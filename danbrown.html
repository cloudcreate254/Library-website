The Da Vinci Code - By Dan Brown

The Da Vinci Code

The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown is a gripping thriller that follows Robert Langdon, a Harvard symbologist, and Sophie Neveu, a cryptologist, as they investigate a murder at the Louvre Museum. The story weaves together codes, religious history, and secret societies, taking readers on a fast-paced journey through Europe. With its twists and mysteries, it challenges the boundaries between fact and fiction.
