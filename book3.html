The Pillars of Earth-By Ken Follet

The Pillars of Earth By Ken Follet

The Pillars of the Earth by Ken Follett is a historical fiction masterpiece set in 12th-century England. It revolves around the construction of a grand cathedral in the fictional town of Kingsbridge. The story intertwines the lives of a builder, a monk, a noblewoman, and a young outlaw, exploring themes of ambition, love, power, and survival. Rich in historical detail, the novel paints a vivid picture of medieval life while weaving a gripping tale of personal and political struggles.
