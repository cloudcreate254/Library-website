The Brain-By David Eagleman

The Brain-By David Eagleman

*The Brain: The Story of You* by David Eagleman is a fascinating exploration of how the brain shapes our identity, perceptions, and experiences. Blending neuroscience with storytelling, Eagleman examines topics like decision-making, memory, and the nature of consciousness, making complex ideas accessible and engaging. The book invites readers to understand the science behind what makes us who we are.
