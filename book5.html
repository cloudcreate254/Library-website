The Theory of Everything- By Stephen Hawking

The Theory of Everything- By Stephen Hawking

The Theory of Everything by Stephen Hawking is a concise and accessible exploration of the universe's origins, structure, and fundamental laws. Based on a series of lectures, Hawking covers topics like the Big Bang, black holes, and the nature of time, offering profound insights into the cosmos while making complex ideas understandable to a general audience.
