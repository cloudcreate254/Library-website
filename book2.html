The-Alchemist By Paulo Coelho

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a timeless fable about Santiago, a young shepherd who embarks on a journey to find treasure in the Egyptian pyramids. Along the way, he discovers the importance of following his dreams, listening to his heart, and recognizing the signs of destiny. Filled with wisdom and inspiration, it’s a story about self-discovery and pursuing one’s personal legend.
